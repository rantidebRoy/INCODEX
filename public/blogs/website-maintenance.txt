Launching a website is just the beginning; ongoing maintenance is crucial to ensure its continued success. Regular website maintenance involves updating content, monitoring performance, and addressing technical issues to provide a seamless user experience. 

Key aspects of website maintenance:
1. Security Updates: Keeping your CMS and plugins up to date.
2. Content Updates: Keeping information fresh and relevant.
3. Performance Monitoring: Ensuring fast load times.
4. Backup Management: Regular backups to prevent data loss.
